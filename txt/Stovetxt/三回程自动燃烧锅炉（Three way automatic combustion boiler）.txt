三回程自动燃烧锅炉
庞龙三回程自动燃烧锅炉、采用欧洲最新燃烧技术组合我国煤种和燃烧技术，均衡的多次配风方式，往复折返换热等先进技术，该锅炉热效率高，消烟除尘效果好，环保节能。
Panglong three way automatic combustion boiler adopts the latest combustion technology in Europe to combine the coal and combustion technology in China, balanced multiple air distribution mode, reciprocating turn back heat exchange and other advanced technologies. The boiler has high thermal efficiency, good smoke and dust removal effect, environmental protection and energy saving.
